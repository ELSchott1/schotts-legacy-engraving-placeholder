About Schotts Legacy Engraving

Schotts Legacy Engraving was built around careful craftsmanship and respect for the stories behind each stone. From memorial work to address markers and personalized gifts, every project is approached with patience, precision, and a commitment to lasting quality.

Local Service, Lasting Work

Locally owned and operated by Eric Schott, Schotts Legacy Engraving proudly serves families, homeowners, cemeteries, and local communities throughout Northeast Florida and South Georgia.

Each project begins with listening: the wording, the stone, the setting, the deadline, and the details that matter. The goal is simple: thoughtful engraving that feels right and lasts.
